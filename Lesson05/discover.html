Discover the Tri-Hamptons

Northampton Community Park

Northampton Township

The Northampton Township Community Park is known for it's playgrounds, basketball courts, and outdoor hockey rink which is the main hotspot in the park. Along with it's baseball and soccer fields it also home to the Northampton Miracle League, a baseball league for individuals with intellectual and physical disabilities.

Northampton Parks and Rec website

Tammanend Park

Upper Southampton Township

Tammanend Park is a park full of fun activities, a spot for nature hikes, and sports. It is also home to the Southampton Days Carnivals that occur every year on the week of the Fourth of July.

Tamamened Park website

Bowlero Bowling and Arcade

Lower Southampton Township

Bowlero is a family fun center with 48 bowling lanes, laser tag, an arcade, and a bar and grill. Many different parties and events can be hosted here as well.

Bowlero Feasterville website

The Iron Oven

Upper Southampton Township

The Iron Oven is a family-friendly restaurant. It has many delicious appetizer and meal orders. From it's deep dish pizza, to it's breaded chicken tenders and mozzarella sticks, no other meal will be as delicious! It also hosts reservations for big groups or post-party events.

The Iron Oven website

The Valley of Fear

Lower Southampton Township

Do you have what it takes to conquer your fear? Every year in October prior to Halloween, this family-fun attration hosts haunted mazes with jumpscares, zombies, and other creepy scares. Invite family and friends to come to this event that only happens once a year.
