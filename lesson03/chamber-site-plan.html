Bucks County Pennsylvania Tri-Hampton Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Purpose

The website for the Tri-Hampton Chamber of Commerce will help local businesses talk to each other. It will show information on member, activities, events, and how to join.

Scenarios

Angelo's Pizza

Angelo's Pizza is a member of the local chamber of commerce. They are opening a second location inside the tri-Hampton boundaries. They would like to have a grand opening ribbon-cutting ceremony to promote the new location. The chamber will help them organize and perform the event and have local township leaders there to show they support the business.

Local Doctor Offices

There are a lot of doctor offices in the tri-Hampton community. They want to have retreats two times a year to have discussions on their practices and new research. They connect with the Tri-Hampton Chamber of Commerce to look for locations that would offer discounts for their events.

Family Request to open their own Supermarket

A local family would like to open up a produce market in the tri-Hampton area. They were made aware of small business grants offered by the tri-Hampton Chamber of Commerce and have applied for the money.

Natural Goodness Foods

Natural Goodness Foods, an organic food store, is opening in the area. They would like to be mentored on how to market their business to the people in the tri-Hampton area. They would like to join the Chamber of Commerce to find a mentor business to help them grow their business.

Stapleston's Hardware Store

Stapleton's Hardware Store is looking for a workshop on workplace safety. The chamber of commerce has these workshops every month. The chamber also has monthly discussion groups with other members of the chamber on the same topic.
